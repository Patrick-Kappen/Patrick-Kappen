How I build

Boring in production.
Interesting in design.

The best systems make their state visible, their changes reviewable, and their failure modes unsurprising.

  1. 01

    Declarative first

    If it matters, it belongs in versioned, reviewable configuration.

  2. 02

    Observable by default

    Logs, metrics, traces, and health signals are part of the design.

  3. 03

    Recovery is a feature

    Backups, rebuilds, and rollback paths matter as much as deployment.

  4. 04

    Small tools, clear boundaries

    Composable components should do one job without hiding state.
